# LMA Demo Script — Q2 Pipeline Review

**Duration:** ~10 minutes
**Format:** Live Zoom meeting with 3 participants + LMA Virtual Participant
**Recording:** Screen capture of Bob's browser (LMA UI) with Zoom audio

| Person | Role | Audio Setup |
|--------|------|-------------|
| **Bob** | VP Sales / Demo Driver | Separate device, clear mic. Operates LMA UI on screen. |
| **Jeremy** | Account Executive | Separate device, clear mic. |
| **Chris** | Solutions Architect | Separate device, clear mic. |
| **Alex** | LMA Virtual Participant (bot) | Already in the Zoom call before recording begins. |

## Stage Directions & Notes

### Screen Layout
- **Primary screen capture:** Bob's Chrome browser, full screen
- **LMA meeting detail page:** Transcript panel (left ~60%), Chat assistant panel (right ~40%)
- **Zoom audio:** All 3 participants + Alex audible throughout
- **No picture-in-picture** of participants' faces needed — the LMA UI is the star

### Key Transitions
1. Title slide → LMA UI (cut, not fade)
2. LMA meeting detail → Meetings List → Meetings Query (navigate within the app)
3. LMA UI → Closing slide (cut)

### Pacing Notes
- **Don't rush Scene 1.** Let the transcript stream for 10-15 seconds before narrating. The visual of words appearing in real time IS the demo.
- **Scene 4 (Salesforce):** The assistant may take 3-5 seconds to respond. Fill the pause naturally: "It's connecting to Salesforce now..."
- **Scene 6 (Voice):** After "Hey Alex", pause and let the bot respond. Don't talk over Alex.
- **Scene 7 (Post-meeting):** Summary generation may take 10-20 seconds. Say "It takes a moment to process the full transcript..." while waiting.

### Recovery Lines (if something fails)
- **Salesforce slow:** "The MCP connection can take a moment on first use — let me show you a result from an earlier session..."
- **Alex doesn't respond:** "Sometimes in a noisy meeting the wake phrase needs a second try — but I can also just type the question..." *(type it in chat)*
- **Translation glitch:** "Let me toggle that again..." *(uncheck and recheck)*
- **Summary not generating:** "The summary typically appears within 30 seconds — while we wait, let me show you the meetings list..."
