Key Capabilities

Section titled “Key Capabilities”

Live Transcription

Real-time speech-to-text with speaker attribution, multi-language support, live translation, PII redaction, and custom vocabulary.

Meeting Assistant

AI-powered Strands agent chat interface with built-in tools, Knowledge Base integration, Guardrails, model selection, and custom prompts.

Transcript Summarization

Automatic meeting summaries, action items, and key decisions generated by Amazon Bedrock during and after meetings.

Voice Assistant

Natural voice interaction with Nova Sonic, ElevenLabs TTS, and optional Simli avatar for face-to-face AI assistance.
